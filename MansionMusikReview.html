Mansion Musik Review- An All Star Flop



Trippie Red's Mansion Musik is a bloated, terribly produced mess with a couple of songs that are alright listens. The amount of ok to goos features do not compensate for this LP's flaws in any way. Mansion Musik is an overall mess and is a terrible album overall.

The production in Mansion Musik is over done with too many drum beats and tries too hard to be something its not. The drum beats are overly repetitive and don't differ and offer any variety. The beats themselves are not inspired in any way and feel like I have heard them before many times. The album seems to be inspired by Playboi Carti's Whole Lotta Red, as well as Chief Keef's Mansion Musick and it is definitely poorly adapted on both fronts. The overall length of the album is too long and there are too many songs that barely differ from each other. The album's 25 songs makes it feel bloated and listening to it is tiring. Trippie's auto-tune is also annoying and it sounds off beat and not well synchronised. The only point I can find to praise is the amount of features that Trippie has in this album. There is not any incentive to listen to it again, as it is tiring, repetitive, uninspired and has terrible production.

I do not think I have heard so many annoying lyrics in any album and the abundance of these terrible lyrics makes it even worse. Firstly, the auto tune makes Trippie's verses completely annoying and repetitive. The lyrics themselves are just plain and bad. There is not a line in this LP that has anything interesting to say or a line that has a good rhyme. Trippie's flow is generic and boring as well. The storytelling from Trippie is basically non-existent and Trippie never tries to tell a story. His delivery never changes and remains the same throughout this entire LP. There is not any emotion in Trippie's delivery and it is just bland. There is no versatility about what Trippie raps about as it consists of money, women and memes. If there is any good lyricism, it comes from a few of the feature artists and even though there are big rap names like Future, Travis Scott, Juice Wrld and Lil Baby, most of their verses are mediocre to good. If I am being honest most of the other features have lines that are bad to mediocre. Overall, the majority of factors I mentioned above add to my reasoning of why this LP is bad.

Mansion Musik is a bloated, horribly produced, badly written and has too many mediocre features. The abundant amount of features does not make up for this album's flaws and instead double downs on these issues. Although I have many issues with this album, there are 2 or 3 songs that are ok listens, but in an album with 26 songs this is unacceptable. Trippie Red's Mansion Musik is a bad album that is not worth anyone's time.
